The MIT Asian Dance Team (MIT ADT) provides the MIT and Greater Boston communities with a taste of East Asian culture via the medium of dance. We perform works from traditional Chinese, aboriginal folk, and contemporary lyrical to Korean hip-hop and Japanese idol dance!

Important News: Our Fall 2013 auditions are on Saturday, September 7th from 3 to 5:30 pm in the
McCormick Hall Dance Studio. Please see the Auditions page for more information!

Recent News

September 7, 2013 - Fall 2013 Auditions!

Interested in joining us this term? Fall 2013 auditions will be held on Saturday, September 7th, 2013 from 3-5:30 pm in the McCormick Dance Studio.

At auditions, you will learn some traditional and hip-hop choreography, as well as preview this semester's pieces. Please wear comfortable clothing that you can move in.

Don't worry if you don't have any dance experience! Many of our members join without prior dance experience. We accept all those who audition, regardless of experience, race, age, gender, etc. (Note: You do not have to be a first-year, or even an undergraduate, to audition. We welcome everyone and would love to have you!)


August 18, 2013 - Summer Workshop #4: Wedding Dress

Our fourth and final workshop will be Wedding Dress, a popular Kpop piece by Taeyang. It will be held on Sunday, August 18th from 7-8 pm in the Walker Memorial Dance Studio (enter Walker Memorial from the Memorial Dr. side, and the dance studio will be on the 2nd floor, to your right). Wedding Dress was performed by ADT in the past, near our founding. Again, the workshop will be a fun and relaxing one-hour dance session, perfect for trying out something new. Just make sure to dress in something comfortable!

Also, remember to sign up for our summer workshop mailing list if you are interested in any of these workshops!

June 16, 2013 - Summer Workshop #1: Ribbon

This summer, we will be hosting a series of workshops in both Kpop and traditional Asian dance. All workshops are open to the general public and will be held approximately every other week in the Walker Memorial Dance Studio.
