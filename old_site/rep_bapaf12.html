Inspirasian - 2012

Inspirasian 2012 was the second annual Boston Asian Performing Arts Festival, hosted by the MIT Asian Dance Team. While many guest groups from all around Boston, including Dance Revelasian, a semi-professional Chinese dance troupe, and Wellesley Wushu, presented acts, MITADT presented many pieces. Among these pieces were kpop medleys and a full-company martial arts series featuring three weapons: the fan, the sword, and the flag.
