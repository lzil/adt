Repertoire

Limitless

The MIT Asian Dance Team Fall 2012 showcase, Limitless, was a 16-piece program beginning with a martial arts (featuring "Ninjas") piece and culminating with a traditional Asian piece entitled "July Torch Festival." Among the other pieces showcased were Yi and Han ethnic dances, and popular Kpop pieces "Genie" (SNSD) and Where U At (Tae Yang).
