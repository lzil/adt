Illusions

The MIT Asian Dance Team's Fall 2013 showcase, Illusions, consisted of two sold out shows! We performed spectacular Dai, Mongolian, and Han ethnic pieces, classical and contemporary dances, as well as hip-hop pieces by VIXX, Nine Muses, EvoL, and Show Luo.
