Dec. 1, 2012 - 'Limitless' Fall 2012 Showcase

We will be having our Fall 2012 showcase on Saturday, December 1, 2012! We will be having two shows on the same day, one from 4-6pm and the other from 7:30-9:30pm. Both shows will be held in Kresge Little Theater.

Come to see us perform spectacular Yi, Han, and Korean athnic pieces, classical and contemporary dances, as well as K-pop pieces by TVXQ, SNSD, U-Kiss, T-ara, and TaeYang!
