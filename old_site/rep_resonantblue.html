Resonant Blue

The MIT Asian Dance Team fall 2010 showcase was named Resonant Blue, after a famous Japanese pop song by girl group Morning Musume). In this 16-piece program, the audience was taken through a journey of the darker side of love, life, and memory, with everything from majestic Mongolian dances to lyrical love songs to gorgeous classical Chinese fan dances to sexy Korean hiphop.
